Comprehensive Finance Problem Statement

The capstone project challenges you to apply concepts from all 5 weeks of the bootcamp to analyze, evaluate, and propose solutions for a complex financial scenario.

This comprehensive assessment will test your understanding of:

  • Financial foundations and systems (Week 1)
  • Time value of money and risk analysis (Week 2)
  • Market mechanics and instruments (Week 3)
  • Advanced derivatives and commodities (Week 4)
  • Business models and FinTech innovation (Week 5)
